AutoFit Data Deletion Request

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This page explains how users of AutoFit (AutoFit Routine) can delete their data. AutoFit has no account, and all data is stored on your device only. No user data is kept on AutoFit servers.

1. App and developer

  • App name: AutoFit (AutoFit Routine)
  • App package: com.motionroutine.app
  • Developer / operator: HANSEON LABS (Owner: Hanseon Oh)
  • Contact email: richanseon@gmail.com

2. How to delete your data

Option A. Delete only some data

  1. Open the item you want to remove (workout routine, workout log, body weight, InBody record, recovery record, photo, custom exercise, etc.).
  2. Delete that item.
  3. The deletion is applied to your device immediately.

Option B. Delete all data

  1. In the app, tap Settings → Reset All Data. This permanently deletes all routines, workout logs, body weight, InBody records, recovery records, and photos at once (cannot be undone), or
  2. Go to your device Settings → Apps → AutoFit → Storage and tap Clear data (clear storage), or
  3. Uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all AutoFit data stored on the device.

Option C. Delete backup files

Files created with backup export are kept in the location you choose. Delete those files yourself if you no longer need them.

If you cannot use the app or need help, email richanseon@gmail.com.
