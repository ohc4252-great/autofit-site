AutoFit Data Deletion Request

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This page explains how users of AutoFit (AutoFit Routine) can request deletion of their account or of some of their data.

1. App and developer

  • App name: AutoFit (AutoFit Routine)
  • App package: com.motionroutine.app
  • Developer / operator: AutoFit operator
  • Contact email: richanseon@gmail.com

2. How to delete your data

Option A. Delete only some data (keep your account)

  1. Open the item you want to remove (workout routine, workout log, body weight, recovery record, custom exercise, etc.).
  2. Delete that item.
  3. If you are signed in, the deleted item is removed from both this device and the cloud (Supabase).

To request deletion of specific data, email richanseon@gmail.com with the items to delete. We process the request after verifying your identity.

Option B. Delete your account and all cloud data

  1. Sign in with the account you want to delete.
  2. Open the Settings tab.
  3. In the Account area, tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm to permanently and immediately delete your account and all cloud data.

Option C. Request by email

If you cannot use the app, email richanseon@gmail.com. Include the app name (AutoFit / AutoFit Routine) and the Google or Apple account email you signed in with. We process verified requests within 30 days.

3. Data deleted, retained, and retention period

CategoryDataHandling
Deleted data
(cloud)		 Account email and user ID, workout routines, workout logs, body weight, recovery records, custom exercises, Pro subscription status Deleted immediately on account deletion / within 30 days for verified email requests. Not retained on AutoFit servers after deletion.
On-device data Progress/workout photos, InBody records, app settings, optional profile Not stored in the cloud. Removed when you delete them in the app or uninstall the app.
Data that may be retained Purchase/subscription receipts and transaction records AutoFit does not collect or store payment method details. Receipts and transaction records are retained by Google Play or the Apple App Store under their own policies and applicable law, not on AutoFit servers.

Signing out alone does not delete cloud data. Use options A–C above to delete data.

4. Contact

  • Email: richanseon@gmail.com
  • First response: within 3 business days
  • Completion: within 30 days after identity verification
