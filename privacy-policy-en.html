AutoFit Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: June 3, 2026

AutoFit Routine (com.motionroutine.app) is a fitness log app that helps you record and manage workout routines, workout logs, body weight, InBody entries, recovery records, progress photos, and workout photos.

AutoFit stores all of your data on your device only. There is no account or sign-in, and your workout and health records are not synced to AutoFit servers or any cloud. The free version may show Google AdMob ads.

  • Service name: AutoFit Routine
  • App package: com.motionroutine.app
  • Operator and privacy contact: AutoFit operator

AutoFit has no account. All of the following stays on your device only. It is not sent to AutoFit servers or backed up to any cloud.

3. Ads and automatically collected information

The free version may show Google AdMob ads. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect or share the following information with Google for ad serving, ad measurement, and fraud prevention.

Advertising IDAndroid Advertising ID (AAID) and, if permitted, iOS IDFA

Whether ads are personalized depends on your region, consent choices, device settings, and Google ad settings. In regions that require additional consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland), AutoFit uses a Google-required consent method (UMP or a Google-certified CMP) before serving certain ads.

You can reset or delete the advertising ID in device settings, and change tracking permission on iOS. AutoFit does not send your workout logs, weight, InBody records, recovery records, or photos to Google AdMob for ad targeting.

5. Payments and subscriptions

AutoFit may offer Pro features. Payments are processed through Google Play or the Apple App Store, and subscription status is managed through RevenueCat (a subscription management service provider). AutoFit does not collect or store payment card numbers or payment method details.

ProviderRoleInformation processed
Google LLC / Apple Inc.Billing, subscription managementPurchase history, receipts, order ID, subscription status
RevenueCat, Inc.Verify purchase/subscription status and restore entitlementsAnonymous app user ID, purchase receipts/subscription status, device/store identifiers

RevenueCat verifies Pro entitlement from store receipts, and the app keeps only the result (active state, expiry) as a cache on your device. No separate account identifier (such as email) is used for payments. Subscriptions may auto-renew each period until you cancel; pricing, renewal period, refunds, and subscription management follow each store's policy and the terms shown at checkout.

6. Health and fitness information

Workout logs, weight records, InBody records, and recovery records may be sensitive health information. This information is processed on your device only and is not used for ad targeting, location tracking, or data sales.

AutoFit is not a medical service. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe for, or prevent any disease or medical condition. App statistics and recovery guidance are for personal reference only. If you have pain, an injury, an illness, or a medical question, contact a qualified medical professional.

7. Retention and deletion

  • Data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • InBody records are limited to 500 entries for app performance.
  • Photos are managed up to 30 on the free plan and up to 3,000 on the Pro plan.

How to delete your data:

  • Delete individual routines, logs, weight, InBody, and photos in the app.
  • Delete all app data: device app info → clear storage/data, or uninstall the app.
  • You manage any backup files you exported yourself.

AutoFit does not store your data on a server, so there is no server-side deletion process. However, uninstalling the app does not automatically cancel a Google Play or Apple App Store subscription. Cancel subscriptions directly in each store's subscription settings.

Backup export does not include photo files. Photos can be saved to your device gallery using the separate gallery export feature. Send requests to richanseon@gmail.com; we verify identity and respond within 30 days.

9. How we protect information

  • Data is stored in the OS app sandbox (/data/data/com.motionroutine.app/).
  • Device backup extraction (adb backup) is disabled (android:allowBackup="false").
  • Workout and health records are never sent to AutoFit servers and are kept separate from ads SDK data (advertising ID, IP, ad interactions).

10. Children under 13

AutoFit is not directed to children under 13. A parent or guardian must supervise and consent to use by a child under 13, and we will guide data deletion on a guardian's request.
