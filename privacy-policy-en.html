AutoFit Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: June 8, 2026

AutoFit Routine (com.motionroutine.app) is a fitness log app that helps you record and manage workout routines, workout logs, body weight, InBody entries, recovery records, progress photos, and workout photos.

In short:

  • Workout, health, and photo records are stored on your device.
  • AutoFit does not provide account sign-in or AutoFit cloud sync.
  • To move data to another device, you must export and import a backup file yourself.
  • Pro purchases and restoration may be processed by Google Play, the Apple App Store, and RevenueCat.
  • If the free version includes an ads SDK, Google AdMob may process information needed to serve ads.
  • AutoFit does not use workout records, health records, InBody records, recovery records, or photos for ad targeting, data sales, insurance, employment, credit, or lending decisions.

3. Information that may be processed off device

3.1 Payments and subscriptions

AutoFit may offer Pro features. Payments are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. Subscription verification and purchase restoration may be handled by RevenueCat.

AutoFit does not collect or store payment card numbers, bank account details, or payment method details.

RevenueCat checks subscription status using an anonymous app user ID, not an AutoFit account email. The app stores only the Pro active state and expiry date as an on-device cache.

Deleting the app does not automatically cancel a paid subscription. Cancel subscriptions directly in Google Play or Apple App Store subscription settings.

5. Information AutoFit does not collect

Under the current app structure, AutoFit does not directly collect or store the following on AutoFit servers:

6. Health and medical notice

Workout logs, body weight records, InBody records, and recovery records may be sensitive health or fitness information. This information is processed on your device and is not used for ad targeting, location tracking, data sales, insurance, employment, credit, or lending decisions.

AutoFit is not a medical service. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe for, or prevent any disease or medical condition. App statistics and recovery guidance are for personal reference only. If you have pain, an injury, an illness, or a medical question, contact a qualified medical professional.

7. Retention and deletion

  • Data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • InBody records are limited to 500 entries for app performance.
  • Progress and workout photos are managed up to 30 on the free plan and up to 3,000 on the Pro plan.
  • Backup files you export are controlled and deleted by you.

How to delete your data:

  • Delete individual routines, logs, weight records, InBody records, and photos in the app.
  • Clear app storage/data in device settings.
  • Uninstall the app.

AutoFit does not store your data on its own server, so there is no server-side deletion process for workout and health records. However, purchase receipts, subscription status, and transaction records may be retained by Google Play, the Apple App Store, and RevenueCat under their own policies and applicable law.

9. Notice for users in the United States

Some U.S. state privacy laws (such as the California CCPA/CPRA) apply only to businesses that meet certain thresholds, such as revenue or processing volume. AutoFit may not currently meet those thresholds, but where those laws apply, you may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or opt-out.

AutoFit does not sell workout or health records or photos, and does not use sensitive health or fitness information for personalized advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising.

Some states, such as Washington, broadly protect "consumer health data." AutoFit's workout, body weight, InBody, and recovery records are processed on your device only and are not transmitted to AutoFit servers, so AutoFit does not collect, sell, or share them.

Consumer Health Data Notice (Washington and similar states)

Some U.S. state laws, such as Washington's My Health My Data Act, broadly protect health-related information — including workout, body weight, body composition (InBody), and recovery records — as "consumer health data." AutoFit handles such information as follows:

  • Collection/retention: AutoFit does not collect or store workout, body weight, InBody, or recovery records on AutoFit servers. All health-related information is processed on your device only.
  • Sources: Information you enter in the app or that is generated on your device (processed on-device only).
  • Sharing/selling: AutoFit does not share or sell consumer health data to any third party, and does not use it for targeted or cross-context behavioral advertising. There are no third parties or affiliates with whom such data is shared.
  • Your rights: You can view, edit, and delete health-related records directly in the app, and remove all of it by clearing app data or uninstalling. You may also send requests to richanseon@gmail.com.

10. Safeguards

  • Data is stored in the operating system app sandbox.
  • Android backup extraction through adb backup is disabled with android:allowBackup="false".
  • Workout and health records are not sent to AutoFit servers and are kept separate from ads SDK data.
  • Purchase entitlements are checked through store receipts and RevenueCat results; payment card details are not processed in AutoFit.
  • Backup files are outside the app once exported, so you are responsible for storing them safely.

11. Children

AutoFit is not directed to children under 14 in Korea or under 13 in the United States (per COPPA). If a child under that age uses the app, a parent or guardian must supervise and consent to that use. On a guardian request, we will guide deletion steps.
