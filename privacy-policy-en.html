AutoFit Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: June 2, 2026

AutoFit Routine (com.motionroutine.app) is a fitness log app that helps you record and manage workout routines, workout logs, body weight, InBody entries, recovery records, progress photos, and workout photos.

AutoFit is device-first. If you do not sign in, your records stay on your device. If you sign in with Google or Apple and agree to cloud sync, only selected records are stored on Supabase. The free version may show Google AdMob ads.

1. Who operates the app

  • Service name: AutoFit Routine
  • App package: com.motionroutine.app
  • Operator and privacy contact: AutoFit operator
  • Email: richanseon@gmail.com

2. What information the app stores

2.1 If you do not sign in

Workout routines, workout logs, body weight, InBody records, recovery records, photos, app settings, optional profile details, and purchase status stay on your device.

2.2 If you sign in and agree to cloud sync

Information stored in the cloudWhy it is used
Google or Apple account email and user IDTo identify your signed-in account
Workout routines, workout logs, body weight records, recovery recordsTo back up and restore selected records on another device
Custom exercises and Pro statusTo restore exercises you created and Pro access

Workout logs, weight records, and recovery records may be sensitive health-related information. AutoFit asks for separate consent before syncing these records to the cloud. If you do not consent, you can still use all features in guest mode.

2.3 Information that is not sent to cloud sync

Not sent to cloud syncDetails
InBody records and InBody CSV dataKept on your device. They may be included only if you manually export a backup file.
Progress photos and workout photosNot backed up to our server. Photos are saved to your device gallery only if you choose the gallery export feature.
App settings and optional profile detailsUsed on your device.

3. Ads and automatically collected information

The free version may show Google AdMob ads. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect or share the following information with Google for ad serving, ad measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

InformationDetails
Advertising IDAndroid Advertising ID (AAID) and, if permitted by the user, iOS Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)
IP address and approximate locationIP address may be used to estimate general location, such as country or city. AutoFit does not collect precise GPS location for ads.
App interactionsApp launch, ad impressions, ad clicks, video ad views, and similar ad-related interactions
Device, app, and diagnostic informationDevice type, operating system, app version, ads SDK performance, errors, load time, and fraud or abuse signals

Whether ads are personalized depends on your region, consent choices, device settings, and Google ad settings. In regions where additional consent is required, such as the EEA, the UK, and Switzerland, AutoFit must use a Google-required consent management method, such as UMP or a Google-certified CMP.

You can reset or delete the advertising ID in your device settings. On iOS, you can change tracking permission settings. AutoFit does not sell or share workout logs, weight records, InBody records, recovery records, or photos for ad targeting.

6. Health and fitness information

Workout logs, weight records, InBody records, and recovery records may be sensitive health or fitness information. AutoFit uses this information to provide the features you request and, if you agree, to back up and restore selected records. AutoFit does not use this health or fitness information for ad targeting, location tracking, or data sales.

AutoFit is not a medical service. App statistics and recovery guidance are for personal reference only.

7. U.S., Canada, and other users

If local law gives you additional privacy rights, we will handle valid requests after verifying your identity and to the extent required by law. You may ask us to confirm, correct, delete, export, or limit certain uses of your information.

We do not sell consumer health data. We do not share consumer health data for targeted advertising. AdMob advertising IDs and ad interaction data are handled separately from your health and fitness records.

8. Retention and deletion

  • Data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • InBody records are limited to 500 entries for app performance.
  • Photos are managed up to 30 photos on the free plan and up to 3,000 photos on the Pro plan.
  • If you use Delete Account in the app, your account and cloud data are deleted.
  • Deleting your account or uninstalling the app does not automatically cancel a Google Play or Apple App Store subscription. Cancel subscriptions directly in the relevant app store settings.

9. Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, export, or withdrawal of consent by emailing richanseon@gmail.com. We will verify your identity and respond within 30 days.

10. How we protect information

  • Data is sent over encrypted connections.
  • Supabase cloud data is separated by user.
  • Database security rules prevent other users from accessing your records.
  • InBody records and photos are excluded from cloud sync.
  • Ads SDK data is handled separately from AutoFit's workout and health record database.

11. Children under 13

AutoFit is not directed to children under 13. If a child under 13 uses the app, a parent or guardian must supervise and consent to that use.

12. International processing and storage

  • Supabase sync data is stored in the Seoul, South Korea region (AWS ap-northeast-2).
  • Supabase, Google, and Apple may operate as foreign companies or use global infrastructure.
  • Google sign-in, Apple sign-in, Google AdMob ads, Google Play billing, and App Store billing are handled under each provider's policies and infrastructure.

Provider policies: Supabase, Google Privacy Policy, Google advertising technologies, Apple Privacy
